FINDING YOUR FILES
====================

Your personal files live in your Home folder — open the Files app (the
file manager icon in your taskbar/dock) and it opens there by default.
Inside, you'll find familiar-looking folders:

  Desktop      — what's shown on your desktop, including this folder
  Documents    — general files
  Downloads    — anything downloaded from a browser lands here
  Pictures     — photos and screenshots
  Music, Videos — media

There's no separate "C: drive" — everything is under one folder tree
starting at "/", and you almost never need to think about that. Your Home
folder is the one that matters day to day.

Plugging in a USB drive or SD card mounts it automatically and shows it in
the file manager's sidebar, the same as Windows or macOS. Safely remove it
from there before unplugging (right-click -> Eject, or the eject icon next
to its name).
